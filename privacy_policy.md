# Privacy Policy for EasyEmails

**Last Updated: April 17, 2026**

EasyEmails ("we," "our," or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
EasyEmails is designed to be a **privacy-first** tool. 
*   **Local Processing**: Extracted email addresses, page titles, and URLs are processed and stored locally. 
*   **Gumroad Integration**: If you choose to upgrade to Pro, your license key is sent to Gumroad's API (`api.gumroad.com`) solely for the purpose of validating your subscription. No personal extraction data (emails, URLs) is ever sent to Gumroad or any other server.

### 2. Data Storage
*   **Local & Sync Storage**: We use `chrome.storage.local` to store your extraction history and `chrome.storage.sync` to keep your preferences (like appearance and ignore lists) consistent across your devices. 
*   **Zero Server Storage**: We do not maintain a database of your captured emails. Your data lives on your device and within your encrypted Google Chrome profile.
*   **User Control**: You have full control over your data. You can clear your entire extraction history or individual entries directly from the side panel or options page at any time.

### 3. Third-Party Sharing
We **do not** share, sell, or rent your data to any third parties. Outside of the license verification request to Gumroad, the extension makes no outbound network calls containing user settings or data.

### 4. Permissions
EasyEmails uses the following permissions:
*   `storage`: To save your extracted emails and settings across sessions.
*   `sidePanel`: To provide a dedicated sidebar for managing your contacts.
*   `<all_urls>` (Host Permission): To allow the extension to identify and extract emails on the websites you visit.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of the extension and this document.

### 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the official support channels on the Chrome Web Store.

---

**By using EasyEmails, you agree to the practices described in this policy.**
